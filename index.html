  • Welcome to Reasonable Faith's Singapore Chapter!

    We are a Singaporean apologetics ministry featuring the work of philosopher and theologian Dr. William Lane Craig in order to:

    1. provide an articulate, intelligent voice for biblical Christianity in the public arena.
    2. challenge unbelievers with the truth of biblical Christianity.
    3. train Christians to state and defend Christian truth claims with greater effectiveness.

    We usually have monthly meetings on the 1st Weds of each month at 8pm. This is held at Graduate Christian Fellowship Conference Room, 420 North Bridge Road #05-04, 188727. We will announce the meetings on our Facebook Group and post it here.

    We also provide churches and ministries apologetics training through conducting courses and giving talks. Please contact us if interested.
